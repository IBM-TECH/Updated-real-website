A four-frame key art set for the opening sequence of an unannounced RPG. The brief: "make it feel like the city is breathing — like every window is somebody's whole life."

I built the city block procedurally in Houdini (15 modular building kits, three height profiles, four density passes), lit it in Octane with one hero key and a haze pass, and overpainted in Photoshop for the final mood. The neon signage is custom — every glyph was hand-drawn so the language reads as a real script rather than visual noise.
